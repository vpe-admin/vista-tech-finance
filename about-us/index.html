About Us

The purpose of VTF is to serve as the captive leasing arm of our holding company so our Managed Services and Enterprise customers can quickly and easily make strategic technology investments while also avoiding major capital outlays.


VTF also provides short term rentals to our customers who may have temporary needs for various technology assets.


While each company within the VPE family has its own unique value proposition, we intentionally structured each business to complement one another and help make our customers technology consumption strategy easier.
