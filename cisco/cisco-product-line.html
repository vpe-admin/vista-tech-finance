VTF Cisco Product Lines

Cisco Catalyst Switches

  • Vista Technology Finance offers Cisco’s current portfolio of Catalyst Series switches. This includes the Catalyst 9200, 9300, 9400, 9500 and 9600 switches.
  • The current Catalyst line replaces the Catalyst 3850, 3650 and 2960X/XR Series switches, which are now all End-of-Life and End-of-Sale.
  • The 9000 series Catalyst line incorporates Cisco DNA Center, which allows Cisco customers to simplify management and streamline network operations.

Cisco UCS

  • Vista Technology Finances’ go-to compute offering are the Cisco UCS rackmount C-Series and blade B-Series servers
  • Cisco UCS helps increase server performance, flexibility, and scale for virtualized and non-virtualized applications
  • Cisco UCS also helps minimize the number of adapters, cables, and access-layer switches needed for server LAN and SAN connectivity
  • Cisco UCS Manager is the only server management system that allows you to manage both blade and rack servers from a single interface

Cisco Meraki

  • VTF’s go-to wireless offering is Cisco Meraki
  • Cisco Meraki provides a single pane of glass to manage your network. All of your endpoints, devices, security and firewalls can be configured from a single interface in the cloud. Rather than managing all those devices and settings at an individual level, you can manage them via Meraki’s Cloud Management dashboard
  • Meraki devices are exceptionally scalable. Adding devices to your network is simple via the user interface on the management dashboard

Cisco Small Business Switches

  • VTF offer’s Cisco’s full line of Small Business switches
  • The Cisco Business Dashboard and mobile app simplifies network operations and automates lifecycle management
  • The Small Business portfolio provides ultimate flexibility from 5 to 48 ports and Power over Ethernet combinations. Flexible pricing models that complement every stage of business growth
  • Through advanced security features, you’re able to prevent network breaches and segment network traffic
