Meraki


VTF’s go-to wireless offering is Cisco Meraki


Cisco Meraki provides a single pane of glass to manage your network. All of your endpoints, devices, security and firewalls can be configured from a single interface in the cloud. Rather than managing all those devices and settings at an individual level, you can manage them via Meraki’s Cloud Management dashboard


Meraki devices are exceptionally scalable. Adding devices to your network is simple via the user interface on the management dashboard
